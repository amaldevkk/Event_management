Our process

How we work with you

  1. Enquiry: You contact us via call, WhatsApp or the website form with your event date, location and type.
  2. Planning: We suggest stage design, sound & light options and share sample photos / ideas.
  3. Quotation: We prepare a clear quote with package details and add-ons (LED wall, DJ, special entry, etc.).
  4. Execution: Our team comes to the venue, sets up everything and supports during the event.
  5. Wrap-up: After the event, we safely dismantle and clear the stage and equipment.
